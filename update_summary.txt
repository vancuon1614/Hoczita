BAO CAO CAP NHAT GIAO DIEN (UPDATE SUMMARY)

1. TONG QUAN
Cac thay doi trong ban cap nhat tap trung vao viec dieu chinh giao dien, dong bo font chu Baloo 2 toan he thong, tang giam kich co chu hop ly, va dong bo van phong mo ta do kho giua cac tro choi.

2. CHI TIET CONG VIEC DA THUC HIEN

2.1. Thay doi Font chu toan he thong (GoogleFonts.baloo2)
- Da chuyen toan bo cau hinh textTheme trong file lib/core/theme/app_theme.dart sang GoogleFonts.baloo2.
- Viet script (update_fonts.dart) de quet toan bo thu muc lib/**/*.dart, tu dong thay the cac tu khoa "TextStyle" va "GoogleFonts.outfit" sang "GoogleFonts.baloo2".
- Xu ly triet de tat ca cac loi bien dich (173 loi) lien quan den tu khoa "const" bi xung dot sau khi cap nhat sang GoogleFonts.baloo2.
- Lenh da su dung:
  + dart run update_fonts.dart
  + (Get-ChildItem -Path lib -Recurse -Filter *.dart) | ForEach-Object { (Get-Content $_.FullName) -replace 'const GoogleFonts.baloo2', 'GoogleFonts.baloo2' | Set-Content $_.FullName }
  + dart fix --apply

2.2. Dieu chinh kich co chu (Font Size)
- Buoc 1: Giam dong loat 2 don vi (fontSize - 2) cho toan bo ung dung thong qua script update_fonts.dart. Cac co chu tu 6 tro xuong duoc giu nguyen de dam bao hien thi.
- Buoc 2: Tang 1 don vi (fontSize + 1) danh rieng cho 4 "trang ben ngoai" (outer pages) thong qua script increase_outer_fonts.dart de giup de doc hon, bao gom:
  + lib/features/home/views/main_screen.dart
  + lib/features/learn/views/learn_tab.dart
  + lib/features/game/views/game_tab.dart
  + lib/features/profile/views/profile_tab.dart
- Lenh da su dung:
  + dart run increase_outer_fonts.dart

2.3. Dong bo van phong mo ta do kho cac man choi
- Dong bo noi dung mo ta (subtitle) cua 3 do kho (Khoi dong, Tap trung, Thu thach) trong 2 tro choi Sắp xếp từ vựng (Word Scramble) va Ô chữ tiếng Anh (English Crossword) de giong voi van phong cua Ô chữ toán học (Math Crossword).
- Noi dung da doi:
  + Khoi dong: "Lam quen nhe nhang voi..."
  + Tap trung: "Tang cuong thu thach voi..."
  + Thu thach: "Danh cho nguoi choi nang cao voi..."
- Cac file da thay doi:
  + lib/features/game/views/english_crossword_game_screen.dart
  + lib/features/game/views/word_scramble_game_screen.dart

2.4. Cai thien loi bien dich
- Phat hien va sua cac loi Invalid constant value trong file lib/features/game/views/multiple_choice_game_screen.dart.
- Phat hien va sua loi goi sai ten AnimatedDefaultTextStyle trong lib/features/home/views/main_screen.dart.
- Lenh kiem tra: 
  + flutter analyze (ket qua: no issues)

3. QUAN LY FILE TAP LENH (SCRIPTS)
- Cac script chay 1 lan de fix font chu (update_fonts.dart, increase_outer_fonts.dart) da duoc xoa bo de don dep du an do khong con nhu cau tai su dung.

4. QUAN LY MA NGUON (GIT)
- Tao nhanh (branch) moi voi ten "new_design" de luu tru phien ban cap nhat giao dien nay.
- Lenh da su dung:
  + git checkout -b new_design
  + git add .
  + git commit -m "UI: Apply Baloo 2 globally, adjust font sizes, sync difficulty descriptions"

5. BIEN DICH UNG DUNG (BUILD APK)
- Lenh da su dung:
